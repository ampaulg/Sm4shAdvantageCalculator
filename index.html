This calculator makes the following assumptions:

  • Grounded moves and projectiles hit on their first active frame. If you hit later in the animation (which is likely for projectiles) you will have more advantage.
  • Aerial moves do not autocancel, and hit on the frame before you land. Your result will vary if you autocancel and/or you hit earlier.
  • The defender does not powershield

For multihits, make sure you use the last hit's values to get the right results (ex. the second hit of ZSS up tilt and not the first).
